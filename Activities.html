Programs

Robotics - In progress

Offline training initiative focused on hands-on learning in robotics, Arduino, electronics, sensors, and IoT. The program covers key concepts like building and programming Arduino-based systems, creating sensor-driven projects, and developing IoT solutions. Participants engage in practical workshops and collaborative challenges, gaining essential skills in robotics integration and embedded systems. Emphasizing creativity and problem-solving, this program equips aspiring innovators to turn ideas into impactful solutions, fostering a new generation of tech-savvy leaders prepared to advance technology and drive change.

Gharbiya Hack CLub Program - Closed Now

online initiative designed to empower students with essential skills in web development, competitive programming, and cybersecurity. Through interactive sessions, participants dive into coding challenges, build dynamic websites, and explore the foundations of cybersecurity to understand and protect digital environments. Emphasizing collaboration and problem-solving, this program provides hands-on projects and real-world applications, equipping young innovators with the expertise needed to excel in today’s digital world and preparing them for future tech-driven challenges.

Global Hack Club Work Shops - Open Now

Platform offers a collection of beginner-friendly, hands-on coding projects designed to empower students to build real-world tech skills. Created by students for students, the workshops cover a variety of topics, from web development and game design to creative coding and hardware hacks. Each workshop provides step-by-step instructions, making complex topics approachable and fun. By the end of each project, students create fully functional applications, games, or tools they can share with others. With an emphasis on creativity, exploration, and community, Hack Club Workshops is a go-to resource for young innovators eager to learn and create.

Type racing - Closed Now

Exciting and fast-paced event designed to test participants' typing speed, accuracy, and focus. In this competitive environment, typists of all skill levels race against the clock—and each other—to complete given text passages with the highest accuracy and speed possible. The competition challenges participants to improve their typing proficiency while fostering a friendly spirit of competition. With timed rounds, real-time rankings, and prizes for top performers, the Type Racing Competition offers a fun and engaging way to sharpen typing skills, build concentration, and achieve new personal records.
