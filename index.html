What is Gharbiya Hack Club?

🚀

Learn By Building

We're not just another coding class. At Gharbiya Hack Club, you'll learn by building real projects, solving real problems, and creating things that matter to you.

🌍

Global Community

Join thousands of Hack Clubbers worldwide. Connect with fellow hackers, share your projects, and be part of something bigger than yourself.

🎯

Expert Mentorship

Learn from experienced mentors who are passionate about helping you grow. Get personalized guidance on your projects and career path.

💡

Free & Open

Everything we do is completely free. No hidden costs, no paywalls. Just pure learning, building, and fun.
