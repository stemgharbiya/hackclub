## Workshop Overview

The **Toppings YSWS**, created by @shambhavi and sponsored by Jared for Hack Club. Members added more CSS *flavour* to their websites to earn toppings for boba or ice cream.

### What members learned

- **CSS Grids** — Making fun, flexible layouts
- **Flexboxes** — Arranging content with ease
- **CSS Variables** — Writing cleaner, reusable styles
- **Responsive Design** — Layouts that adapt to screen size
- **CSS Animations** — Bringing sites to life
- **Margins & Padding** — Spacing and positioning
- **Gradient Backgrounds** — Adding colour depth

### Requirements

- One animated element
- A responsive change using `@media`
- Use of containers
- Use of a grid
- A gradient background **or** a flexbox (or both!)

### The Reward 🍨

Members who met all requirements earned **$5 to buy toppings** for their boba or ice cream.

### Outcome

The workshop generated **$150 in income** for the club, supporting future activities and events.
