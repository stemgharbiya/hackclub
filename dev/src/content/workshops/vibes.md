## Workshop Overview

The **Vibes YSWS** (You Ship, We Ship) program, sponsored by Jared — Club Operations Lead at Hack Club. Members built live websites with assistance from AI and good vibes.

### The Three Parts

**🧠 Part One — Prompt an AI** to code a website about a topic of your choice.

**🎨 Part Two — Edit the code yourself** to change styling and content.

**🚀 Part Three — Add new features** to improve your website and make it your own.

### The Reward 🍕

Once members finished all three parts, the club celebrated together with pizza (or another snack chosen by the leader).

### Outcome

The workshop generated **$65 in income** for the club, supporting future activities and events.
