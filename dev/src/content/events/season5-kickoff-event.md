## Event Overview

The inaugural season 5 event served as an introduction to **HackClub** and our branch. The kickoff showcased bootcamp offerings and featured competitive activities including a **competitive programming (CP) challenge**.

## Blue Team vs Red Team Competition

The highlight was an engaging **blue team versus red team competition** centered around web security:

- **Blue Team Objective**: Secure accounts by enabling SQL injection protection through a toggle mechanism
- **Red Team Objective**: Exploit SQL injection vulnerabilities and capture secrets from organizer-created accounts
- **Challenge Focus**: A vulnerable website featuring SQL injection vulnerabilities on the login page

## Event Conclusion

The event concluded with **sticker distribution** to all participants.
